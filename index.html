About PMX Malaysia

Building Critical Digital Infrastructure for Malaysia

PMX Malaysia is a fully integrated data centre solutions provider with deep roots in Malaysia's digital infrastructure landscape. We don't just build data centres — we design them for efficiency, build them for resilience, and operate them for the demands of tomorrow.

From hyperscale campuses in Johor's Sedenak Tech Park to enterprise facilities across the Klang Valley, our DBO+ methodology delivers best-in-class outcomes for operators, investors, and hyperscalers entering the Malaysian market.

Our Core Methodology

Design, Build & Operate — The DBO+ Difference

Most providers do one thing. PMX Malaysia does everything under one roof — reducing risk, eliminating handover gaps, and giving you a single accountable partner from concept to live operation.

D
Design

Bespoke data centre design tailored to your power density, cooling requirements, and growth targets. Our in-house engineers deliver optimised layouts that reduce CAPEX without compromising Tier III/IV resilience.

B
Build

End-to-end project delivery with in-house civil, M&E, and IT infrastructure teams. On-site value engineering, rigorous commissioning, and proven track record on hyperscale campuses exceeding 100MW capacity.

O+
Operate & Beyond

24/7 facilities management, security, and continuous optimisation. Our operators are embedded from day one of design — ensuring the facility they manage is one they helped engineer.
